The Steps for Making Dosa.

  • Wash and soak rice and Urad dal seperately
  • Grind them separately into a smooth batter using a little water. The urad dal batter should be fluffy.
  • Mix the rice and urad dal batters together in a large bowl.
  • Cover the batter with a clean cloth and let it ferment in a warm place for 8-12 hours or overnight. The batter should double in volume and become slightly sour
  • Adjust consistency
  • Heat a dosa griddle (tava) on medium-high heat.
  • Grease the griddle with oil or ghee.
  • Pour a ladleful of dosa batter onto the hot griddle.
  • Spread the batter into a thin, even dosa using the back of the ladle.
  • Cook until the dosa turns golden brown and crispy on the bottom.
  • Optional toppings/fillings (e.g., potato masala, chutney, sambar)
